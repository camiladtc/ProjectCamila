We connect you with the greatest puppy options according on your needs, personality, and treatment through our adoption service. Dog and Owner Activities

Join our group of comfort dog owners for activities that will benefit you both. From walks and basic training to bonding sessions and tea-treat time, we provide a full calendar of activities for dog and owner. We're here to help you, and we're excited to meet you! Know more about it.

Counselling

On request, we offer free mental health counseling sessions with our qualified therapists. We offer a caring, confidential and professional service. You can always rely on us! If you are interested please do not hesitate on reaching out to us through our contact page.
