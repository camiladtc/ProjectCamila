Which dog breed suits you best?

This fluff ball is extremely affectionate and loves to cuddle. The King Charles Spaniel is known as "The Love Sponge" because of its cuddling nature. Because of their sweet nature and eagerness to please, these puppies are said to be easy to teach and fantastic with children.

Labs are friendly, outgoing, and high-spirited. They are highly athletic dogs that love being outdoors, which encourages their human companions to do the same.

Poodles are super social, hypoallergenic dogs and love being in the company of their owners. They share the same people-pleasing personality traits that Labs do, and the Poodle's high level of intelligence qualifies them for a variety of service dog jobs.

Few dog breeds have the ability for love and loyalty like Golden Retrievers do. They're also known for being docile and well-behaved. They're extremely friendly, intelligent, and devoted to their human companions.

Yorkies are one of the most loyal, affectionate, and trainable breeds due to their tendency to form strong bonds with their human. Because Yorkies are so small, they're a particularly good dog to have if you live in an apartment and don't have access to a yard.

Viszlas create very close ties with their owners and hate being left alone, which makes this buddies wonderful companions for anyone suffering from depression.
